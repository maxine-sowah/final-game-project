Anjeza keeps calm. After all she needs to keep a level head for this heist to go smoothly. She no longer wants to take any more chances. Liam and Harley split up to find the man. The hostages all scream, upon hearing a gunshot. Anjeza and the robbers are also stunned to hear this, but stay cool.

Liam and Harley return with the hostage and Liam tends to his injury. Anjeza advices the hostages to never try to be the hero...
