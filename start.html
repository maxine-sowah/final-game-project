The Royal Heist

Anjeza lives in the land of Winterfell. She is a distant relative of the Royal Family, but was exiled from the castle. As a result, she is left with absolutely nothing. No food. No money. Nothing. Anjeza is angry. As revenge she decides to do do a heist with four other people who have nothing to lose. She also wants to send her family a message. These four accomplices are Jordyn, Harley, Liam, and Benjamin.

Will this be a successful heist or will they get caught?
